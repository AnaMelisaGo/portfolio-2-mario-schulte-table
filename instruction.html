How To Play Mario Schulte table

  1. Click start button to play. Random numbers are shown in the table.
  2. Fix your eyes at the center cell of the the game table with blue borders.
  3. Find and click the numbers in ascending order. Start from 1 up to 25.
  4. Try not to move your eyes away from the center and look for the numbers using peripheral vision.
  5. If the sequence is correct, the box turns green and if it's not correct the box turns red.

Recomendations

  • Choose a comfortable place before playing (for better concentration)
  • The distance from the screen to your eyes should be aproximately 30 -35cm (same as when reading a book)
  • Try not to count the numbers aloud.
  • Do not overdo to avoid fatigue. It's recommended to train once or twice a day for 10min
